# Tidal Ink Studio - Project Brief

## Business Overview
Tidal Ink Studio is a modern, bright tattoo and piercing studio located on the Mount Maunganui waterfront in the Bay of Plenty, New Zealand. Founded in 2023, the studio breaks away from traditional dark, grungy tattoo parlor aesthetics, offering a clean, coastal-inspired environment where artistry meets the ocean.

## Business Details
- **Location**: 142 Marine Parade, Mount Maunganui, Bay of Plenty 3116
- **Founded**: 2023
- **Owner**: Sarah Te Whare & Marcus Chen
- **Specialties**: Custom tattoos, traditional Polynesian tā moko, body piercing, cover-ups
- **Hours**: Tuesday-Saturday 10am-6pm, Sunday 11am-4pm, Closed Mondays
- **Phone**: +64 7 574 8900
- **Email**: hello@tidalink.nz

## Mission Statement
"Where the ocean meets the ink" - Creating meaningful, beautiful body art in a welcoming coastal environment that honors both contemporary and traditional tattooing practices.

1. **Showcase artistry**: Gallery of work from all three resident artists

3. **Cultural education**: Dedicated tā moko section explaining cultural significance
4. **Streamline bookings**: Easy consultation booking with image upload
5. **Local SEO**: Capture "tattoo Mount Maunganui" searches

- Consultation bookings via contact form
